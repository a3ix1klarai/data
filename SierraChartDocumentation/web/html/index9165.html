BitMEX Trading Service

  • Introduction

Introduction

[Link] - [Top]

Sierra Chart still maintains integration for BitMex for trading in current versions of Sierra Chart. At some point this may be removed. There is no technical support provided. And there is no documentation provided any longer. The BitMex trading integration should not be used by new users.

Sierra Chart supports market data for crypto currencies from various exchanges. Refer to Cryptocurrency Data Services.

There are no plans for trading support on crypto currency exchanges. They do not meet our technical requirements and we have no interest in providing technical support for them. Sierra Chart has its plan to develop our own exchange. Additionally we are looking to support decentralized trading.
